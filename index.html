  • Completely Free & Super duper simple
    Easy-to-follow instructions every step of the way. Navigate with the TAB key or by clicking the back/continue buttons, and follow your progress towards completion with a bar at the top of the screen. We won't charge you, ever.
  • Compatible With Chrome
    Be sure you're using Google Chrome. It might work in Firefox. It probably wont work on mobile, and definitely wont on Internet Explorer.
  • Procrastination Station
    Did you find something more fun to do when you were making your resume? Go ahead, we'll automatically save the text that you leave in the fields so you can come back later.
  • We aren't thieves
    Nothing you enter on this site is ever sent to us or saved on any servers, so nobody sees it except you.
  • Because making pretty resumes isn't one of your skills
    You can see an example of the beautiful default theme here, but if you want to do more, you can also create your own using HTML and CSS. Learn more.
  • We're not done yet!
    I'm a single full-time student who has been working on this between classes for less than a week! I'm still working on ironing out all the bugs, so if you find any, or have any suggestions, please shoot me an email @ mitch@mitchs.co.
